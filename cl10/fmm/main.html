MUTUAL FUNDS

  • Mutual fund is a special kind of investment through which you can invest in different types of securities together.
  • It is a collective investment scheme pooling money from many investors.
  • It is managed by AMC who appoints expert fund managers.
  • Investors can invest in stocks, bonds and other assets.
  • It helps you in diversified investment and reduce individual risk.

Asset management company (AMC)

  • Opens mutual fund.
  • Appoints expert and through their advice, they invest money at different places.
  • This AMCs keep 1 to 2% profit and returns, rest to its investors.
  • If AMC invest in your money in
    1. Stocks - high risk - high return
    2. Government bonds - low risk - low returns
  • HDFC,HSBC,ICICI , Aditya Birla, Tata, etc., are the companies/banks who open mutual fund.

Benefits of mutual funds

  • Small investment
  • Professional fund management
  • Spreading risk
  • Transparency
  • Choice

Net asset value (NAV)

  • Net asset value is the price at which you buy or sell mutual funds unit and it changes everyday.
$$ \text{NAV} = \frac{\text{Total Assets} - \big(\text{Total Liabilities} + \text{Expenses}\big)}{\text{Total Outstanding Units}} $$

Q: Find NAV, if assets is equal to 10 lakh, liabilities = 1 lakh, expenses of 2% of assets per annum and total number of units are 90000.

A:

$$ \text{NAV} = \frac{10,00,000 - (1,00,000 + 20,000)}{90,000} = \frac{8,80,000}{90,000} \approx 9.78 $$

Risk involved in mutual funds

  • Market risk : factors such as economic condition, government policies, interest rate and investor sentiments can influence the fund. Overall performance of the market can influence the mutual funds also.
  • Non market risk: the potential gain and risk in a portfolio which is non related by the movement of the market in general. Bad news of an individual company can bring down it's stock prices which can negatively affect the fund holding.
  • Interest rate risk :
    1. Bond price and interest rate moves in opposite direction.
    2. When interest rate increases Bond prices fall.
    3. The decline in underline asset affect the one negatively
  • Credit risk : chances that the borrower doesn't repay the loan or fulfill loan obligation. It leads to the fall in the value of the bond causing net asset value of the fund to take a beating.
